Pila Ski Resort

The Pila ski resort is nestled in the Aosta valley region, of northern Italy. From the town of Aosta you can take a cable car directly to the base of the resort. The Pila ski resort sits at an altitude of 1,814 meters (5,951 feet) above sea level, and the highest pistes (ski runs) will take you to an altitude of 2,700 meters (8,858 feet)! While there you will find that you have plenty to do with a total of 24 ski runs including:

  • Four blue runs
  • Twenty nine red runs
  • Four black runs
  • Pipezone
  • Slopestyle zon
If your looking for more information about the Pila Ski Resort checkout: https://pila.it/en/
